About ESAF Balajyothi Club

A dynamic framework committed to nurturing well-rounded, responsible, and empowered students across Kerala & Tamilnadu. Rooted in values of community service, leadership, and academic enrichment, the program has evolved through its club-based model, leadership training initiatives, summer camps, and a wide range of developmental activities. Each component is carefully designed to guide children not only through their academic journey but also through personal and social growth.
